Create a modern, dark-themed eCommerce footer section for a brand named “Thunderbolt”. The design should feel premium, minimal, and slightly futuristic with clean spacing, subtle hover effects, and responsive layout.

Structure the footer in a well-balanced multi-column layout (3–4 columns on desktop, stacked on mobile).

Include the following sections:

1. Brand Section:

* Thunderbolt logo (text-based is fine)
* Short tagline (e.g., performance-driven tech products)
* Keep it minimal and bold

2. Quick Links:

* Home
* Shop
* Categories
* About
* Contact

3. Customer Support:

* Help Center
* FAQs
* Shipping Info
* Track Order

4. Policies:

* Privacy Policy
* Terms & Conditions
* Returns & Cancellation Policy

For the Returns & Cancellation Policy page/modal:

* Write it in a professional, clear tone
* Include logic such as:
  • Orders are first verified via a confirmation call by admin
  • Once confirmed, orders cannot be cancelled during processing/shipping
  • After delivery, return/cancellation requests may be accepted for valid reasons
  • A small fixed cancellation/return fee (₹50) may apply
* Keep wording user-friendly, not harsh or robotic
* Format it cleanly (headings + short paragraphs)

5. Contact Info:

* Add a realistic dummy address (India-based)
* Email ([support@thunderbolt.com](mailto:support@thunderbolt.com))
* Phone number format (+91 XXXXX XXXXX)

6. Social Media:

* Only include Instagram icon (clean minimal icon style)
* Add hover animation

7. Trust Element:

* Add a subtle “secure experience” indicator (like a small shield icon with text such as “Secure Shopping Experience”)

8. Footer Bottom Bar:

* Copyright line (© 2026 Thunderbolt. All rights reserved.)

Design Guidelines:

* Dark UI (black/charcoal background)
* Soft contrast text (off-white/gray)
* Smooth hover transitions
* Clean typography (modern sans-serif)
* Proper spacing and alignment
* Mobile-first responsiveness

Avoid:

* Newsletter section
* Payment method icons
* Cluttered layout

Let the design feel premium, clean, and slightly cinematic.
